6) Cookies and Fingerprinting

a) Anybuild may use cookies or fingerprinting for users of our website, these are used to identify returning users of our site, they can make the experience of using our site more convenient for users because the system remembers previously entered information such as preferences for that particular user.

  i) A cookie is a small text file which is stored by the Browser on your computer's hard drive. Anybuild typically just users a session cookie which is deleted when the user finishes their session. The session cookie is for the user’s convenience enabling the users to changes pages within the Application without constantly relogging in. If Anybuild uses a permanent Cookie tit will ask for the User's permission.

  ii) As an alternative to Cookies, Anybuild may uses a technique called fingerprinting - this collects information from the Browser such as Browser Version, Fonts installed, Screen resolution, IP address etc. Anybuild can and remember this combination of attributes which is usually unique for a particular user and device in order to recognize that user as a returning user.

b) We use cookies and fingerprints to identify which pages are being used. This helps us analyze data about web page traffic and improve our website in order to tailor it to customer needs. We only use this information for statistical analysis purposes and then the data is removed from the system.

c) Overall, cookies and fingerprints help us provide you with a better website by enabling us to monitor which pages you find useful and which you do not. A cookie in no way gives us access to your computer or any information about you, other than the data you choose to share with us.

d) You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies and fingerprints if you prefer. This may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website.
